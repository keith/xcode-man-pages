NAME

avbdeviced —
peristent entity management for AVB.

SYNOPSIS

avbdeviced

DESCRIPTION

The avbdeviced executable is used for the managment of persistent AVB entities which are advertised by the Mac.

avbdeviced is launched automatically by launched as needed on demand and should not be launched manually.
